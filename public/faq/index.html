Q: What is KPM?
A: Kappas per minute.
Q: How is KPM calculated?
A: It is a rolling average. A kappa count only lasts for 1 minute.
Q: How does it count Kappas?
A: Twitch chat is built on top of IRC. It uses a bot to join/part the relevant channels.
Q: How often does the site update?
A: Every 5 seconds.
Q: OMG my Kappas aren't being counted.
A: Sorry, the IRC connection isn't always best and the bot may currently be disconnected from your chat.
Q: How is the average calculated?
A: It tracks the total number of Kappas since recording over the total duration it has recorded.
Q: How long has the site been live?
A: The recording started around 5AM EST on March 8th, 2015.
Q: Is there an API?
A: There is only one at the moment. Once the leaderboards are built, there will be more. Currently, there is: /api/get/data which is what the site uses.
